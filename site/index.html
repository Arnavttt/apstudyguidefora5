Five & A+

Five & A+ was founded by Arnav Sinha and co-founded by Yashwin Kandra, a Don Bosco Prep student from New York in the Class of 2027. After taking on 11 AP® courses and coding for years, Yashwin wanted to help build a study system that worked for students who do not fit perfectly into conventional study habits.

This is not just flashcards, and it is not a blank Notion page that leaves all the organizing to you. The goal is to put lessons, videos, practice, explanations, and review paths in one place, so students can spend less time building a study system and more time actually learning.
